# Privacy Policy
This website service collects only the information necessary to provide essential features of a mailing list.
### Information collected
When signing up, the following information is required:\
-First name\
-Email address\
Optionally, subscribers can enter:\
-Last name
### How this information is used
Name and email address are used only to send intentional, infrequent updates about releases or announcements related to this ongoing project. Information is never sold, rented or otherwise provided to any third party for any reason other than assisting in communicating with the subscriber.
### Data storage and security
Information is stored securely with standard digital safeguards. GDPR compliance is assured and reasonable measures are taken to prevent unauthorised access, misuse, or loss of data.
### Third parties
Data is sent to and processed solely by an email service provider for the purpose of sending emails. No other third parties have access to this information.
### Your rights
Subscribers have the right to:\
-Access the information stored\
-Request correction or deletion of information<br>-Unsubscribe at any time
### Data retention
Information is retained only while the user remains subscribed. All data connected to the user is removed upon cancellation of the subscription.
### Contact
Questions or requests regarding your data can be directed to [www.tstfie.ch/contact](/contact).

This policy is subject to update if operational changes occur. The most current version will always render on this page.
